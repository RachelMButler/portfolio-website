About Me

Hi! I’m Rachel Butler, a botanist turned user experience designer. I’m motivated by the challenge to create products that have a positive impact on people’s lives. With my scientific background, I have expertise in research and data analysis and I use data driven solutions to build products from conception to hand-off that are intuitive, elegant, and inclusive. I want to join a user experience design team where I can use my skills to contribute to products that are a pleasure to use.
